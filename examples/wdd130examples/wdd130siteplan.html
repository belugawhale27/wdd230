Overview

Purpose

Our main focus is safety, and a fun experience is also important! With that in mind, our goal is to help provide you with the knowledge and equipment to have an adventure that you can live to tell. With safety as our priority, we’ll prepare you with life jackets that fit properly and all that you need to be aware of while on the river rapids. Our team is knowledgeable and readily available to handle any questions or concerns you may have. After we’ve discussed the necessary safety procedures, we’ll prepare you for an unforgettable adventure on the river rapids.

Audience

Whether you’re on vacation or a local, you’re invited to join us on the river rapids! Are you looking for adventure in the great wide somewhere that’s just around the riverbend? Are you looking to enjoy some family time in nature? Do you need some fresh air with a dash of fun? From our family to yours, to all the adventurers, and to anyone who desires some adventure in their lives, we’ve got you covered! Whether you’re enjoying family time, pushing your physical limits, or just enjoying the river rapids, we can help you maximize your time on the river!

The Site Map of a site is just like it sounds…it is a map of the pages in a site and how they are related and linked together. From the map above we can see that we will eventually have the Home page and 2 sub or child pages.

The lines that connect them all together indicate that each page should be accessible from any other page, it is essentially showing us the global navigation for the site.

Wireframes

Wireframes are like blueprints for making webpages. They should show the major sections of content that will be on the page and the relative locations of each element. In the wireframe below you can see there will be 6 sections to our page:

  1. At the top we have a section with the logo (the box with the mountain means an image) and the navigation bar.
  2. Then there is a banner image that stretches all the way across the screen.
  3. Next we have some text and an image
  4. ...followed by another row made up of an image and some text.
  5. Then one more section of text with no image.
  6. Lastly, a footer containing a copyright/name line and 3 social media icons.
